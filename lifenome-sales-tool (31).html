📚 How to Use This Tool
×
Step 1 — Fill Out the Cover Page
1
Enter Your Organization Details
Type your organization name and work email address in the required fields. These personalize the catalog view and generate your solution summary. The website field is optional.
2
Enter Your Partner Access Password
Enter the partner access password provided by your Lifenome point of contact. This is required to access the product catalog. If you don't have a password, reach out to your Lifenome representative.
3
Select Your Organization Type(s)
Choose the organization type(s) that best describe your company — e.g. Longevity Clinic, Health App, Meal Delivery, Food & Beverage Brand. Select multiple if relevant. This tailors which health verticals are shown as primary recommendations.
4
Fill In the Optional Profile Fields
Select your annual revenue range, company stage, expected data volume, dev team size, and primary product interest. These help our team tailor a proposal. Then click Explore Your Precision Health Verticals.
Step 2 — Select Verticals & Data Sources
5
Select a Health Vertical
Use the left sidebar to browse health verticals (Longevity, Fertility, Menopause, Precision Medicine, Meal Delivery, etc.). Your organization type pre-selects the most relevant ones. Click a vertical to focus, or click All Categories to see the full 14-vertical catalog.
6
Choose Your Data Sources
Use the right panel to select which data inputs your platform supports: Blood Biomarkers (Standard / Advanced / Elite tiers), DNA (Genotyping / Exome / WGS), Microbiome (16S or Shotgun by body site), Wearable, EHR, Epigenetics, and more. Select multiple simultaneously.
Step 3 — Explore Insights
7
Browse Insights by Type
Insights are organized into three sections: 💡 Recommendations (personalized program & product plans), 📊 Health Indices (composite scores and dashboards), and 🔬 Biomarker Assessments (individual readings with risk levels). Click each section header to expand or collapse it.
8
Expand Individual Insight Cards
Each card shows only the source badge and insight name by default. Click the card name or the ▼ arrow to expand the full description, output unit, assessment range, and the personalization biomarkers used.
9
Search & Filter
Use the search bar to find insights by name, keyword, or biomarker. Use the filter chips (Recommendations / Health Indices / Biomarker Assessments) to show or hide types. The Analytical Scope bar shows total genomic and clinical coverage of your selected sources in real time.
Step 4 — Build Your Solution
10
Add Insights to Your Solutions Cart
Click the ✓ checkbox in the top-right of any insight card to add it to your Solutions Cart. The cart collects selected insights and generates the corresponding API endpoint configuration for your technical team.
11
Review API Endpoints
At the bottom of the insights panel, the Solutions Cart — API Endpoints block shows the Lifenome API calls corresponding to your selected data sources. Use the Send Summary button to email a full configuration to your team, or Export Cart to download a JSON spec.
💡 Pro tip: Start by selecting your organization type on the cover page — this pre-configures the most relevant verticals. Then add data sources one at a time to see how the insight catalog expands. The Analytical Scope bar at the top shows your total coverage in real time.
