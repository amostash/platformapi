Step 2 — Sample collection

Two parallel collection streams — blood draw and DNA saliva/buccal kit — with flexible location options to match the patient and partner setting.

🩸
Blood draw — three location options
At MedSpa — draw performed on-site at the prescribing partner location. Scheduled lab visit — patient directed to a CLIA-certified partner lab near them. At home — mobile phlebotomy dispatch for eligible panels. Fasting requirements communicated to patient automatically.
🧬
DNA saliva kit (home)
OGR-575 saliva collection for DNA sequencing. Prepaid return envelope. Collection takes 5 minutes. One-time only.
📦
One-time DNA, lifetime utility
DNA panel is collected once. All future peptide candidacy decisions for that patient draw on the same genomic data — no re-collection ever required.

Step 3 — Lab processing & scoring

Lifenome's proprietary scoring engine converts raw blood values and genotype data into composite clinical indices.

Blood processing
CLIA-certified partner lab runs peptide-specific panels. Results transmitted via HL7/FHIR to Lifenome API within 2–4 days.
Genomic analysis
DNA sequencing QC → variant calling → PRS computation → variant classification (ACMG criteria). Covers both common and rare variants relevant to each peptide's safety and efficacy profile.
Composite scoring
Peptide-specific algorithms combine blood + genotype into Candidacy Index, Safety Gate status, Response Predictor tier, and Monitoring Protocol assignment.
